Our Story

Built for Kitchens, Driven by Trust

Since 2021

Simplifying Hospitality Sourcing

At FreshPort, we simplify the way hospitality businesses source their food supplies.

Founded in 2021, FreshPort works closely with hotels, restaurants, cafés, cloud kitchens, and QSRs across Pune, helping them maintain consistent quality, smooth operations, and dependable inventory. We collaborate with trusted manufacturers and brands to ensure kitchens receive the right products, at the right time, without unnecessary complexity

Our product range spans 800+ food and kitchen essentials, including dairy products, sauces, spices, chicken specialties, frozen snacks, syrups, and canned goods. Every category is curated to meet the everyday demands of professional kitchens while maintaining consistency in taste, safety, and performance.

What defines FreshPort is our responsiveness, reliability, and operational understanding of hospitality kitchens. With the trust of 250+ food businesses, we focus on timely deliveries, flexible ordering, and dependable supply — so kitchens can function without interruptions.

From daily-use ingredients to specialised products, FreshPort supports hospitality teams behind the scenes, allowing them to focus on what matters most: delivering great food and memorable dining experiences.

FreshPort — built for kitchens that value consistency, speed, and trust.
